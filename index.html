Welcome to my webpage!

I am a Senior Research Economist at De Nederlandsche Bank. My research interests lie at the intersection of monetary economics, heterogeneous agent models and financial frictions.

I obtained my PhD from the Faculty of Economics at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Elisa Faraglia. Before joining Cambridge, I completed the MPhil in Economics at the University of Oxford and the BSc in Economics and Politics at the University of Bath.
